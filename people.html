People

Principal Investigator

Brent Minchew

Professor of Geophysics
Email: bminchew@caltech.edu
Curriculum Vitae · Google Scholar

Brent is a Texan and (former) United States Marine. He received B.S. and M.S. in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin and a Ph.D. in Geophysics from Caltech in 2016. He held an NSF Postdoctoral Fellowship at the British Antarctic Survey working with Hilmar Gudmundsson before joining the MIT faculty in January 2018. He was promoted to associate professor with tenure in 2024, served as chair of the Program in Geophysics at MIT, and joined Caltech as a Professor of Geophysics in 2025. Brent is also co-founder and chief scientist of the Arête Glacier Initiative.
